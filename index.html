Latest News

Ankara, also known as African wax print fabric, continues to be a vibrant and integral part of African fashion. Renowned Nigerian designer Lisa Folawiyo has significantly influenced its global appeal. In a recent interview, she discussed her journey in transforming traditional Ankara into contemporary designs, gaining international recognition. Celebrities like Lupita Nyong'o and Issa Rae have donned her creations, highlighting the fabric's versatility and elegance.
